WITNESS STATEMENT

Name: Ms. Priya Sharma
Age: 28 years
Occupation: Shopkeeper, Shop 24, Inner Circle
Recorded by: SI Rakesh Kumar on 13-07-2024 at 09:30 hrs

I was standing at the counter of Shop 24 on 12-07-2024 at around 21:30 hrs
when I noticed a man in a grey hoodie behaving suspiciously near the
customer standing just outside my shop. I saw the man reach toward the
customer's rear pocket and immediately run away toward Palika Bazaar. The
customer shouted "stop thief". I preserved the CCTV footage from my shop's
camera and have provided it to the investigating officer.

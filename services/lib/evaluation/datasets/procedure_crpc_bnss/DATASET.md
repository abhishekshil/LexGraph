# procedure_crpc_bnss

**Purpose**: verify that the system correctly locates procedural provisions
under both the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (CrPC) and its successor, the
Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita, 2023 (BNSS), and produces the right
crosswalk.

**Items**: information to police (FIR), arrest without warrant, remand,
bail — classic procedural provisions that are frequently cross-referenced.

**Gold**: structured mappings between CrPC and BNSS sections, plus the
expected section tokens that must appear in the retrieved pack.

**License**: dataset is synthetic; mappings derive from the Govt. of India
published concordance.

# offence_ingredients_ipc_bns

**Purpose**: verify that the system correctly identifies the ingredients of
classic offences under both the IPC (old law) and its BNS equivalent (new law)
and cites the right statutory section.

**Format**: JSONL. See `items.jsonl` for questions and `gold.jsonl` for
expected retrievals.

**Status**: Phase-1 ships a tiny illustrative seed. Phase-5 expands this to
cover 30+ common offences with gold citations from the authoritative Govt.
concordance.

**License**: dataset is synthetic; source-of-truth mappings derive from the
Govt. of India concordance (public domain).

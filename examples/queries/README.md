# Example queries

After `make seed-example`, try these against `POST /api/query`:

1. `What are the ingredients of theft under Section 378 IPC, and what is the corresponding BNS section?`
2. `Which judgments interpret Section 302 IPC?`
3. `What is the procedure for arrest without warrant under Section 41 CrPC, and where is it in the BNSS?`
4. `Compare the admissibility of electronic records under Section 65B IEA and Section 63 BSA.`
5. `Which exhibits in my matter support the allegation of dishonest intention?` (with `matter_scope=matter_demo`)
